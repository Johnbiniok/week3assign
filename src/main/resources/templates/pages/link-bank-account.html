How to Invest

It is highly suggested regardless of the exchange you choose, that you link a bank account for transfers. This is important because it can save you money in the form of fees, and some exchanges may also give out incentives to link a bank account, such as free cryptocurrency or bonuses on your first transfer. It is recommended that you take some time before investing to look at the markets and compare them with the last day, week, month, year, and all time. This will give a good indication of the volatility of that kind of cryptocurrency and a general idea of where it is at in its "cycle".

Most cryptocurrency will fluxuate in price very quickly, however some are more easily predicted than others. The easiest way to predict the future of a coin is by looking at correlations between that currency and another. The two types of correlations are positive and negative. Positive correlation means the currencies will move together whereas negative correlation means the currencies will most likely do the opposite of each other.

How to buy CryptoCurrency

Once your account is set up on any crypto exchange, all you have to do is link a bank account or credit card and you can be the owner of digital currency! It is instantaneous to transfer money (depending on your bank) and you will receive your cryptocurrency immediately. Most exchanges make it pretty self explanatory on how to purchase cryptocurrency, but if you find a brand new kind of coin that was just released on a platform, it is beneficial to check it out early, because that means it will likely gain value.

How to sell CryptoCurrency

Exchanges make it just as easy to sell cryptocurrency as they do to buy it, and it is also instantaneous. This is a large reason it might be a preferred method of investing, because money doesn't need to be tied up for long periods of time between buying and selling.
