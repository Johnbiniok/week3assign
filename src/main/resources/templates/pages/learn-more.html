Learn More

Cryptocurrency is a very complex topic. In order to understand the inner workings of how the prices go up and down, you will need to research, listen to podcasts, and watch price changes. Experience is important when it comes to investing in cryptocurrency so you can't be afraid to lose a little bit of money in order to figure out what you need do! Some investments may lead to more knowledge gain than monetary gain, but in my opinion, that is the easiest way to learn how to start making money.

Podcasts

There are quite a few cryptocurrency websites where they hold regular podcasts, either on the current state of things or possibilities for the future. These can be a great tool to help cement your knowledge, or learn new things! Forbes has done an excellent job gathering some of the best podcasts here. Personally, however, I prefer to listen to coindesk podcasts, which seem to be made well with credible information and sources.

News

There are several sources for news as well. You can find a list of reliable news at either coindesk.com or cointelegraph.com. These sources are specifically for the latest news on cryptocurrency. You can also find a lot of news on other investment websites as well, but these websites are geared specifically for cryptocurrency, so they will have the most accurate news and predictions.
